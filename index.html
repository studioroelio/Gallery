studioroelio is the creative
outlet of Roel Schiffers.
Roel is a self-taught artist whose
paintings reimagine moments from his
daily life and travel. Working from his
own photographs, he transforms these
everyday moments with bold color, layered
patterns, and a sense of fun. His imagery
often balances architectural detail with
imagined arrangements of objects, blurring
the line between observation and memory.
